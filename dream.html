Dream Animation

For the midterm project in our MDIA 3187 - Visual Effects class, we were asked to pick a topic and create an animation that showcases our understanding and application of using keyframes, animation curves, expressions, nested compositions, shape layers and masking. After coming up with a topic, we had to storyboard our animation before executing it in Adobe After Effects. All vectors used inside the animation were created in Adobe Illustrator and imported over.
