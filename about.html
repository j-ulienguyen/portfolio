About Me

Hi, I'm Julie!

I’m a Multidisplinary Designer and Front-End Developer based in Vancouver, BC.


I’m currently a student at the British Columbia Institute of Technology working towards a 2 year diploma in Digital Design & Development.

Before I continue, let’s cut the formality. (That’s what LinkedIn is for — and because I prefer to talk more casually)

My Story

Let’s start with how I even started designing. Well… it probably started around the time I was in grade 4/5 and saw my brother using Photoshop to create banners. Obviously, being the youngest sibling you always want to copy what your oldest siblings are doing because you think it’s cool. And you guessed it! I copied him and that’s how my design journey began.

To be honest, I didn’t think I would pursue a career in design. Prior to this journey, I attended the Beedie School of Business at Simon Fraser University thinking that I would concentrate in Marketing. During this time, I was fortunate enough to get a graphic design internship at a local dessert chain that made me realize a few things.

These were basically my thoughts:

  • I don’t really like this program and the idea of taking irrelevant electives is meh
  • You know what? I’m going to take the year off from school, work full-time, and figure out what I actually want to do
  • Alright, I’m going to drop out of SFU
  • My parents are going to kill me…
  • I love designing

... And now here I am!
