The Collective Coffee Roasters & Bar

The Collective Coffee Roasters & Bar is a fictional coffee shop located at the heart of Gastown, Vancouver. With a strong passion for coffee, The Collective strives to provide the best coffee experience tailored to your tastes.


The final project for Term 1 of our MDIA 1106 - Design Fundamentals class we had to choose a type of business and create a brand out of it. The deliverables included: Site Wireframe, Website Design Mockup, Logo, and Brand Style Guide. We were given the option to try and code out our website for bonus marks despite having 1 term of coding knowledge. Although it is not responsive, you can check it out below (To be honest, it only looks good on my screen).
